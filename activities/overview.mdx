<Info>
The Activities page in disQo.ai provides a centralised, at-a-glance view of all your interactions, events, and outputs across the platform. Easily track what has happened, when, and with which tool.
</Info>

<Frame caption="Activities dashboard UI">
    <img src="/images/activities-dashboard.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

- Filter activities by tool (e.g., Test Data Generator, Automation Scripts Generator, Test Case Generator, Requirement Analyser)
- Switch between <b>Agents</b> and <b>User</b> activity views
- Search and filter to quickly find relevant events

<Tip>
Use the Activities page to audit your actions, troubleshoot issues, and maintain a clear record of your work in disQo.ai.
</Tip>
