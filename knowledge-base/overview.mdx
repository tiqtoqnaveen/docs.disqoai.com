<Info>
The Knowledge Base in disQo.ai allows you to centralise all your important documents, files, and data sources in one accessible location. Easily upload, organise, and sync information from platforms like Jira and Confluence to power your automations and workflows.
</Info>

## Getting Started with Knowledge Base

<Steps>
<Step title="Navigate to Knowledge Base">
    Select <b>Knowledge Base</b> from the sidebar to access your repository of documents and integrations.
    <Frame caption="Knowledge Base main page UI">
        <img src="/images/knowledge-base-main.png" alt="" />
    </Frame>
</Step>
<Step title="Upload Files or Folders">
    Click <b>Upload</b> to add files or folders. You can drag and drop, or browse to select files. Maximum single file size: 5MB.
    <Frame caption="Upload files or folders UI">
        <img src="/images/knowledge-base-upload.png" alt="" />
    </Frame>
</Step>
<Step title="Sync Integrations">
    Use <b>Sync integrations</b> to connect and import data from Confluence or Jira.
    <Frame caption="Sync integrations UI">
        <img src="/images/knowledge-base-sync.png" alt="" />
    </Frame>
</Step>
<Step title="Connect to Confluence or Jira">
    If prompted, connect your Confluence or Jira account to enable syncing.
    <Tabs>
    <Tab title="Confluence">
        <Frame caption="Connect to Confluence UI">
            <img src="/images/knowledge-base-connect-confluence.png" alt="" />
        </Frame>
    </Tab>
    <Tab title="Jira">
        <Frame caption="Connect to Jira UI">
            <img src="/images/knowledge-base-connect-jira.png" alt="" />
        </Frame>
    </Tab>
    </Tabs>
</Step>
</Steps>

## Supported File Types & Limits

| File Type | Extensions                  |
|-----------|-----------------------------|
| Document  | .pdf, .txt, .md, .doc(x)    |
| Data      | .csv, .xml, .json, .xls(x)  |
| Slides    | .ppt(x)                     |

- <b>Maximum single file size:</b> 5MB
- <b>Folders:</b> Supported for bulk upload and organisation

<Tip>
Organise your files into folders for easier management. Use clear, descriptive names for files and folders to improve search and automation accuracy.
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting & Best Practices

<Warning>
If you see a message that your integration is not connected, ensure you have authorised access to your Confluence or Jira account and try again.
</Warning>

- Only upload files you have permission to share.
- Keep your knowledge base up to date by regularly syncing with your integrations.
- Use filters to quickly find relevant documents.
