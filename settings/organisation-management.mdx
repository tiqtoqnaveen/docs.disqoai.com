<Info>
Once you have created an organisation in disQo.ai, the Organisation section will appear in your Settings. Here, you can manage your organisation details, team members, billing, and more.
</Info>

## Accessing Organisation Settings

<Steps>
<Step title="Create an Organisation">
    Click your profile menu and select <b>Create Organisation</b> to start a new organisation for your team.
    <Frame caption="Create Organisation menu">
        <img src="/images/settings-create-organisation-menu.png" alt="" />
    </Frame>
</Step>
<Step title="Manage Organisation Details">
    After creation, access <b>My Organisation</b> in Settings to update your organisation name, avatar, and slug.
    <Frame caption="My Organisation settings UI">
        <img src="/images/settings-my-organisation.png" alt="" />
    </Frame>
</Step>
<Step title="Manage Team Members">
    Use the <b>Team Members</b> section to invite, view, and manage your team. Assign roles and manage permissions.
    <Frame caption="Team Members management UI">
        <img src="/images/settings-team-members.png" alt="" />
    </Frame>
</Step>
<Step title="Billing and Subscription">
    The <b>Billing</b> section allows you to view your subscription plan, manage payments, and see your team size and next payment date.
    <Frame caption="Billing overview UI">
        <img src="/images/settings-billing.png" alt="" />
    </Frame>
</Step>
<Step title="Danger Zone">
    In <b>Danger Zone</b>, you can leave, delete, or transfer your organisation. Use cautionâ€”deleting an organisation is irreversible.
    <Frame caption="Danger Zone UI">
        <img src="/images/settings-danger-zone.png" alt="" />
    </Frame>
</Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Organisation management features are only available after you have created an organisation. Billing and team management options will also appear in your Settings menu.
</Tip>
