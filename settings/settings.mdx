<Info>
The Settings area in disQo.ai allows you to:

• Manage your personal account

• Configure your subscription

• Set up integrations

• Manage your team and billing (if part of an organisation)

All settings are accessible from the sidebar for easy navigation.
</Info>

## What You Can Manage in Settings

- <b><a href="https://app.disqoai.com/settings/profile">My Profile:</a></b> Update your name, email, and personal details.
- <b><a href="https://app.disqoai.com/settings/subscription">Subscription Plan:</a></b> View and manage your current plan, upgrade, or renew your subscription.
  <Note>
  For more details about managing payments and subscriptions, see the <a href="/settings/billing">Billing</a> page.
  </Note>
- <b><a href="https://app.disqoai.com/settings/integrations">Connected Apps:</a></b> Connect and manage integrations with third-party tools (e.g., Jira, Confluence).
  <Note>
  To learn more about integrations, see the <a href="/integrations/overview">Integrations</a> page.
  </Note>
- <b>Organisation Settings:</b> (Visible after creating an organisation)
    - <b>My Organisation:</b> Update organisation details and avatar.
    - <b>Team Members:</b> Invite, view, and manage your team.
    - <b>Billing:</b> Manage your organisation's subscription and payments.
    - <b>Danger Zone:</b> Leave, delete, or transfer your organisation.
    <Note>
    You can create an organisation directly from the Settings area. For step-by-step instructions, see the <a href="/settings/organisation-management">Organisation Management</a> page.
    </Note>
- <b>Appearance:</b> Switch between light and dark mode.
- <b>Sign Out:</b> Log out of your account securely.

<Tip>
Organisation management, team, and billing options will only appear after you have created or joined an organisation.
</Tip>
