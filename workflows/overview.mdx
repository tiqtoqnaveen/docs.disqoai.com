## What are Workflows?
Workflows in disQo.ai let you automate multi-step processes by connecting triggers, actions, and integrations. You can create workflows from scratch or use pre-built templates to get started quickly.

## Getting Started: Creating a Workflow

<Steps>
<Step title="Open the Workflows Page">
    Navigate to <b>Workflows</b> from the sidebar to view and manage your workflows.
    <Frame caption="Workflows Page UI">
        <img src="/images/workflows-dashboard.png" alt="" />
    </Frame>
</Step>
<Step title="Create a New Workflow">
    Click <b>Create new workflow</b> to start. Choose to build from scratch or use a template.
    <Frame caption="Create workflow options (scratch or template) UI">
        <img src="/images/create-workflow-options.png" alt="" />
    </Frame>
</Step>
<Step title="Select a Template (Optional)">
    Browse and select from featured, business analysis, engineering, or QA templates.
    <Frame caption="Template selection UI">
        <img src="/images/template-selection.png" alt="" />
    </Frame>
    <Note>
    For a full list of templates and their details, see the <a href="/workflows/workflow-templates">Templates</a> page.
    </Note>
</Step>
<Step title="Explore Templates">
    View all available templates, including those coming soon, in the Templates section.
    <Frame caption="Templates Overview in Canvas UI">
        <img src="/images/templates-overview.png" alt="" />
    </Frame>
</Step>
<Step title="Design Your Workflow">
    Use the visual workflow canvas to add triggers, actions, and integrations. Drag and drop to build your automation.
    <Frame caption="Workflow Autoamtion Canvas UI">
        <img src="/images/workflow-canvas.png" alt="" />
    </Frame>
</Step>
<Step title="Add Actions">
    Choose from a variety of actions (e.g., send email, fetch Jira tickets, generate test cases) to automate your process.
    <Frame caption="Add Action to Workflow UI">
        <img src="/images/add-action.png" alt="" />
    </Frame>
    <Tip>
    For a detailed explanation of each action and its configuration, visit the <a href="/workflows/actions">Actions</a> page.
    </Tip>
</Step>
</Steps>
