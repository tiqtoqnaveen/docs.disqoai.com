---

<Info>
Billing in disQo.ai is flexible to support both individual users and organisations. The way you manage your subscription and payments depends on your account type.
</Info>

<Tabs>
<Tab title="Individual User Billing">
- Manage your subscription through the <b><a href="https://app.disqoai.com/settings/subscription">Subscription Plan</a></b> page in Settings.
- Use the <b>Manage Plan</b> option to upgrade, renew, or change your plan.
- All payments and invoices are handled at the individual account level.
</Tab>
<Tab title="Organisation Billing">
- If you are part of an organisation, billing is managed centrally via the <b>Billing</b> page in Settings.
- View your organisation's plan, team size, next payment date, and manage payments from this page.
- Only organisation owners or admins can access and update billing details.

</Frame>
</Tab>
</Tabs>

<Tip>
If you upgrade from an individual account to an organisation, your billing will move to the organisation level. For any billing or payment queries, <a href="https://tiqtoq-uk.atlassian.net/servicedesk/customer/portal/4" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">contact our support team</a>.
</Tip>
