## Welcome to disQo.ai

Get up and running with disQo.ai, your AI-powered workflow automation platform for the Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC). Follow these steps to start automating your team's work with intelligent agents and workflows.

### 1. Sign Up or Log In

<CardGroup>
  <Card title="Access disQo.ai" icon="external-link" href="https://app.disqoai.com">
    Visit the disQo.ai platform and sign up for a new account, or log in if you already have one.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### 2. Create Your First AI Agent

<CardGroup>
  <Card title="Explore Agents" icon="bot-message-square" href="/agents/overview">
    Learn about the different types of AI agents available for Business Analysis, Quality Assurance, and Engineering.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Create an Agent" icon="plus-circle" href="https://app.disqoai.com/agents">
    Use the platform interface to create your first agent. Choose a template or start from scratch, and customise it for your workflow.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

#### Start a Chat with an Agent

<CardGroup>
  <Card title="Start a New Chat" icon="message-square" href="https://app.disqoai.com/chats">
    Begin a conversation with any available agent to get instant assistance with your SDLC tasks. Click 'Start new chat', select your agent, and interact in real time.
  </Card>
  <Card title="View Chat History" icon="clock" href="https://app.disqoai.com/chats">
    Review and revisit all your previous conversations in the Chat History. All your interactions are securely stored for future reference.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
